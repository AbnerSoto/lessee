The Lessee

Michael and Lily are a newly wedded couple and they are on their way to building their dream family in a new house they bought. For the time being, they are looking for tenants to rent out the extra rooms available. That is when Oxana enters the lives of this loving couple. There is something intriguing about Oxana, and although a delicate chemistry is been developed between Michael and Oxana, nothing has happened...until Lily's trip away from home to see her mother. The short lived but powerful intimate encounter between Michael and Oxana will bring Michael the internal turmoil that he has never endured before, and Oxana the intimacy that she has been longing all her life.

The Lessee is a story about the internal struggle between temptation and loyalty, the desire to feel companionship, and the fine line between lust and love.
